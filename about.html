Who We Are

Transforming Education Through Hands-On Learning

YantroMitra Learning Technologies Private Limited works closely with schools, educators and students to create meaningful learning experiences through practical application of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics.

Our programs focus on Robotics, Coding, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Drones Technology, 3D Printing,Public Speaking and Innovation Labs.

We believe students learn best when they design, build, experiment and solve real-world problems.
